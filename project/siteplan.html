Site Plan: What to Do in Cumaná

Site Name

Name: What to Do in Cumaná

Reason: This site will showcase the best places, activities, and attractions in Cumaná, making it easier for tourists and locals to explore the city.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a guide to Cumaná's best attractions, including beaches, historical sites, local restaurants, and entertainment spots. The site will be user-friendly and offer a language switcher for English and Spanish.

Scenarios

  • What are the best beaches to visit in Cumaná?
  • Where can I find good local restaurants?
